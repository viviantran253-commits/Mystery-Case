        How to Play

        1. Ask aloudPlayers take turns asking one complete Yes/No question in English.
        2. Host answersThe teacher responds only with Yes, No, Maybe, or Irrelevant.
        3. Earn clue pointsIf a question clearly relates to a secret keyword, the teacher adds one point.
        4. Solve the caseOn a turn, a player may explain who did it, why, and how.

        A player solves the case by explaining the crime correctly and including at least six of the eight secret keywords. One spoken question can earn a maximum of one clue point.
